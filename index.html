ExyCSS - Welcome

Welcome to the main page of the ExyCSS framework ! Here you can discover the functionnalities or read the docs to create your own website with ExyCSS !

Why use it ?

ExyCSS is a newly created framework, and it is very active. Your bugs, ideas or contributions are very important for us.

ExyCSS is simple and it allows you to quickly make a website, with no problems or more CSS...

ExyCSS is flexible : you are not trapped in one solution, the real solution is you. Just view the not-minified version of the framework files and choose what you want to modify.

Where can I start ?

We have a wiki page for our framework who explain you how to setup your website and your components. Be careful on the fundamentals : they are important for the framework comprehension.

If you have any problem, contact us on Discord, Gitter or Twitter.
